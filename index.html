Name of the promoter: If the promoter is Japanese, please provide us with the information both in Japanese and in English.

Company name

Company name in Japanese: The company name in Japanese will be the formal name. As to the company name in Japanese, “kabushiki kaisha”, written in Japanese, i.e., “株式会社”, must be included either at the beginning or at the end of the name. The other part of the company name in Japanese can be either in Japanese characters and/or Roman alphabet. We will provide a Japanese translation of the English name, if necessary.

Company name in English: You can use Kabushiki Kaisha, KK, K.K., Corporation, Co., Ltd. or Ltd. for the English term corresponding to “kabushiki kaisha”(i.e., 株式会社).
